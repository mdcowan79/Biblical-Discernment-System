How BDS Works

The Two Tools

BDX (Quick Check): Fast verdict on any teaching—is it biblical, mixed, distorted, or dangerous?

BDT (Deep Check): Thorough 16-category theological analysis for important decisions.

The 8 BDX Verdicts

  • 🟩 Biblical: Fully consistent with Scripture and the gospel.
  • 🟩 Biblical but Incomplete: True but missing essential context or qualifiers.
  • 🟨 Mixed: Contains both truth and error.
  • 🟨 Distorted: Uses biblical language but reshapes the meaning.
  • 🟨 Speculative: Goes beyond Scripture with imagination or conjecture.
  • 🔴 Unbiblical: Directly contradicts Scripture.
  • 🔴 Deceptive: Appears biblical but designed to mislead.
  • 🔴 Anti-Gospel: Attacks or undermines the gospel itself.

The 16 BDT Categories

  1. Theology Proper: Claims about God's nature and attributes
  2. Christology: Claims about Christ's person and work
  3. Pneumatology: Claims about the Holy Spirit
  4. Trinity: Claims about Father, Son, and Spirit
  5. Bibliology: Claims about Scripture
  6. Anthropology: Claims about human nature and the image of God
  7. Hamartiology: Claims about sin
  8. Soteriology: Claims about salvation
  9. Ecclesiology: Claims about the church
  10. Eschatology: Claims about last things
  11. Angelology: Claims about angels and demons
  12. Covenant & Redemptive History: Claims about God's plan
  13. Christian Identity: Claims about who believers are in Christ
  14. Christian Ethics: Claims about moral living
  15. Christian Practice: Claims about spiritual disciplines and worship
  16. Cultural & Philosophical: Claims about worldview and ideology

What BDS Is NOT

  • Not a way to judge people
  • Not a way to feel superior
  • Not a replacement for prayer, Bible study, or church community
  • Not a tool to win arguments or attack other Christians

What BDS IS

  • A way to stay safe in your faith
  • A way to grow in wisdom and discernment
  • A way to protect your faith and the gospel
  • A way to stay centered on Jesus and Scripture

About BDS

Welcome to the Biblical Discernment System

If you're new to following Jesus, you're going to hear a lot of ideas: sermons, podcasts, TikTok "Christian" advice, YouTube prophets, friends' opinions, cultural messages, and Christian clichés.

Some of it is helpful. Some of it is confusing. Some of it is wrong. Some of it sounds right but isn't.

BDS exists to help you tell the difference—not by guessing, not by feelings, not by "what sounds good," but by Scripture, Jesus, and the Gospel.

The Heart Behind BDS

BDS is built on love, humility, wisdom, truth, grace, and Christlikeness. Discernment is not about being harsh or suspicious. It's about being healthy, wise, safe, and rooted in Christ.

For New Believers

You don't have to know everything. You don't have to be a Bible scholar. You don't have to memorize the whole system. You just need to know:

  • Jesus is the center.
  • Scripture is the standard.
  • The gospel is the filter.
  • And BDS helps you stay on track.

You're not alone. You're growing. And you're safe in Christ.
